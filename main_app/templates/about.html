About Pen & Paper: The Fountain Pen & Ink Tracker

Pen & Paper is designed for fountain pen enthusiasts who want an easy way to organize and document their growing collections. Whether you’re a beginner with a couple of pens or a seasoned collector with drawers full of inks, this app helps you stay on top of your hobby with clarity and ease.

Why Use This App?

  • Stay Organized: Keep track of every pen and ink in your collection without juggling spreadsheets or notebooks.
  • Track Inked Pens: Record which pens are currently inked and with what color, so you never forget what’s in your rotation.
  • Plan Your Pairings: Experiment with different pen-and-ink combinations and make notes on how they perform.
  • Reduce Waste: Know what you already own and avoid accidental duplicate purchases.

Features

  • Catalog your pens with brand, model, nib size, and notes.
  • Organize your inks by brand, color, and type (bottle or sample).
  • Track your currently inked list with start dates, colors, and usage notes.
  • Write down impressions, favorites, and personal ratings to revisit later.

Who Is It For?

This tracker is built for anyone who loves writing with fountain pens. Whether you’re journaling, studying, sketching, or collecting, it helps you connect more deeply with your tools and enjoy the writing experience.

Our Goal

Pens and inks are more than just writing instruments — they are part of a personal journey. This app provides a digital companion to make that journey easier to document, reflect on, and enjoy.
